RFQ details

RFQ details that matter most

Send these details in the first message so Bharat Metals can review the enquiry clearly.

Product form

Mention whether the requirement is pipe, tube, sheet, plate, coil, rod, bar, angle, flat, flange, fitting, fastener, wire mesh, perforated sheet or another material form.

Grade

Mention SS 202, SS 304, SS 304L, SS 310, SS 316, SS 316L, SS 410, SS 420, SS 430 or another grade/specification if known.

Size / thickness / schedule

Pipes need OD and schedule or wall thickness; sheets and plates need thickness and size; rods and bars need diameter or section and length.

Quantity

Share approximate or final quantity in pieces, lengths, sheets, kilograms, bundles, coils or number of line items so availability can be reviewed.

Finish

Mention 2B, No. 1, BA, mirror, matt, hairline, brush, PVC coated, polished or other finish expectations where applicable.

Certificate requirement

Mention MTC, mill certificate or third party inspection requirement early because documentation can affect quotation and sourcing discussion.

Delivery location

Share Chennai area, Tamil Nadu city, South India location, island destination or nearby export destination with transport expectations.

Required timeline

Mention required date, urgency, packing needs and whether transport booking, door delivery or courier for small items should be discussed.

Buyer answers

Frequently asked questions

Answers to common RFQ questions for buyers sending stainless steel, aluminium, brass or copper requirements.

What is the fastest way to request a stainless steel quotation?

Send product form, grade, size, quantity, finish, delivery location and certificate requirement by WhatsApp or email.

Which product forms can be included in one RFQ?

A single RFQ can include pipes, tubes, sheets, plates, coils, rods, bars, angles, flats, flanges, fittings, fasteners, wire mesh or perforated sheets if each item is clear.

What details are needed for pipe and tube enquiries?

Mention grade, welded or seamless preference, outside diameter, schedule or wall thickness, length, finish and quantity.

What details are needed for sheet and plate enquiries?

Mention grade, thickness, sheet or plate size, finish, PVC coating requirement, quantity and certificate need.

Can I send a photo or drawing with the RFQ?

Yes. Photos, drawings or sample references can help clarify size, finish, hole pattern, bend, drilling and processing expectations.

Should I mention MTC or mill certificate in the first message?

Yes. Mention certificate needs at RFQ stage so documentation can be reviewed with the material.

Can transport booking be discussed?

Transport booking, door delivery, courier for small items and export packing can be discussed based on location, quantity and packing suitability.

Can aluminium, brass or copper be included in an RFQ?

Yes. Aluminium, brass and copper requirements can be sent with form, size, quantity and delivery location.

Which payment modes are supported?

UPI, Bank Transfer, Cheque and Cash are listed payment modes.

What are Bharat Metals working hours?

Working hours are 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM, with Sunday as the weekly holiday.
