Buyer answers

Frequently asked questions

Short answers for procurement teams, fabricators and project buyers.

Is Bharat Metals a supplier for Locations We Serve?

Yes. Bharat Metals can review Locations We Serve enquiries from its Chennai base based on stock, sourceability, size, quantity and logistics feasibility.

Which details should I send for a quote?

Send product form, grade, size or thickness, quantity, finish, delivery location, certificate requirement and required date.

Can MTC or mill certificate be discussed?

MTC, mill certificate and third party inspection support can be discussed for applicable stainless steel supplies.

Does Bharat Metals manufacture stainless steel?

No. Bharat Metals uses dealer, stockist, supplier, wholesaler and sourcing support language. It does not claim to manufacture stainless steel.

Bharat Metals is a Chennai stainless steel stockist, supplier and wholesaler established in 1986, focused on practical stainless steel supply across Tamil Nadu and nearby South India markets.
