Stainless steel grades

Stainless Steel Grades

Open grade pages for common stainless steel enquiries.

SS 202

a cost-sensitive austenitic stainless steel grade often discussed for general fabrication and railing work where the application is suitable

SS 304

a widely requested stainless steel grade for fabrication, kitchen equipment, food handling, interiors and general industrial work

SS 304L

a low-carbon 304 variant requested for selected welding and fabrication requirements

SS 310

a heat-resisting grade considered for selected high-temperature applications

SS 316

a molybdenum-bearing stainless steel grade commonly discussed for marine, coastal, chemical and higher corrosion exposure requirements

SS 316L

a low-carbon 316 variant used where welding and corrosion exposure are important

SS 410

a martensitic stainless steel grade requested for selected wear and mechanical applications

SS 420

a martensitic grade sometimes considered for selected hardness and wear-related uses

SS 430

a ferritic stainless steel grade often discussed for decorative, appliance and selected sheet applications

Buyer answers

Frequently asked questions

Short answers for procurement teams, fabricators and project buyers.

Which stainless steel grades are commonly requested for pipes?

Common pipes enquiries include SS 202, SS 304 and SS 316, with SS 304L, SS 316L, SS 310, SS 410, SS 420 and SS 430 reviewed when the requirement suits those grades.

Do you supply SS 304 and SS 316 pipes in Chennai?

Bharat Metals can review SS 304 and SS 316 pipes requirements from Chennai based on size, quantity, finish, documentation and dispatch needs.

What details are needed for a pipes quote?

Send outside diameter, schedule, wall thickness, welded or seamless preference, grade, quantity, finish, delivery location, certificate requirement and required date.

Can MTC or mill certificate be discussed for pipes?

MTC, mill certificate and third party inspection can be discussed for applicable stainless steel pipes supplies.

Do you supply pipes to Coimbatore, Hosur, Trichy and Pondicherry?

Yes. Bharat Metals can review dispatch or transport booking for these locations from Chennai after availability, quantity and packing details are clear.

Which uses are common for stainless steel pipes?

Stainless Steel Pipes are commonly discussed for piping fabrication, utility lines, commercial kitchens, marine repair depending on grade, finish and application.
