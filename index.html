FAQ

Common stainless steel enquiry questions

Is Bharat Metals a stainless steel supplier in Chennai?

Yes. Bharat Metals is a Chennai-based stainless steel stockist, supplier and wholesaler established in 1986, serving buyers across Chennai and Tamil Nadu.

Which stainless steel grades are supplied?

Commonly requested grades include 202, 304, 304L, 310, 316, 316L, 410 and 430, depending on stock, size, finish and quantity.

Do you supply SS 304 and SS 316?

Yes. Bharat Metals supplies SS 304 and SS 316 materials such as pipes, sheets, plates, coils, rods, bars, flanges and fittings based on availability.

Do you provide polished pipes and decorative stainless steel?

Polished pipes, mirror finish, hairline finish, brush finish, decorative stainless steel and PVC coated sheet requirements can be reviewed based on size and quantity.

Do you provide cutting, polishing and PVC coating?

Cutting, polishing, PVC coating, bending, drilling, packing and related processing support can be arranged where suitable for the product and order quantity.

Can you provide MTC or mill certificate?

MTC, Material Test Certificate, mill certificate and third party inspection support can be discussed for applicable stainless steel supplies.

Do you supply to Coimbatore, Madurai, Trichy, Salem and Hosur?

Yes. Bharat Metals supports stainless steel enquiries for Coimbatore, Madurai, Trichy, Salem, Hosur and other Tamil Nadu locations through delivery or transport booking where suitable.

Do you supply to Kerala, Karnataka and Andhra Pradesh?

Bharat Metals can review enquiries from Kerala, Karnataka and Andhra Pradesh with clear product, grade, size, quantity, packing and transport requirements.

Do you handle Sri Lanka and Maldives export enquiries?

Nearby export enquiries for Sri Lanka and Maldives can be reviewed case by case when the product specification, quantity, documentation and packing needs are clear.
